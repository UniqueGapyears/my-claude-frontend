Overview

About This Tour

Walk between two of Nepal's most historically significant and least-visited towns — Bandipur's perfectly preserved Newari hilltop streetscape and Gorkha's royal palace above the clouds. Central Nepal at its most authentic and unhurried.

Highlights

Why This Tour

🏛️

Bandipur Old Town

A perfectly preserved hilltop Newari town — cobbled pedestrian streets, traditional courtyards, and an extraordinary valley panorama.

👑

Gorkha Royal Palace

The ancestral home of Nepal's Shah dynasty — a magnificent hilltop palace above the clouds, barely visited and profoundly atmospheric.

🌄

Himalayan Views

Both Bandipur and Gorkha offer stunning Himalayan panoramas — Manaslu, Himalchuli, and Ganesh Himal visible on clear days.

Day by Day

Your Full Itinerary

All times approximate. Your guide adapts each day to conditions, wildlife, and group preference.

D1
Kathmandu → Bandipur

Kathmandu — Drive to Bandipur

⏰ 4 hours drive

Morning drive west from Kathmandu on the Prithvi Highway, turning off to climb to Bandipur. The town appears suddenly — a ridge of traditional buildings above the valley fog. Check in to a heritage guesthouse and walk the main pedestrian street at dusk.

  • 4-hour drive via Prithvi Highway
  • Arrival in Bandipur (1,030m)
  • Heritage guesthouse check-in
  • First walk along the main cobbled street
  • Sunset views over the Marsyangdi valley
  • Welcome dinner at a traditional Newari home
🏨 Bandipur Heritage Guesthouse
D2
Bandipur

Bandipur Deep Exploration

⏰ Full day

A full day in Bandipur with a local guide who grew up here. Morning: the viewpoint above town with Himalayan panoramas. Afternoon: the cave system below the ridge, the old bahal courtyards, and a cooking class with a local family.

  • 5:30am: Tundikhel viewpoint — Himalayan sunrise panorama
  • Bandipur bazaar — no vehicles allowed
  • Ancient bahal (Buddhist courtyard) exploration
  • Siddha Gufa (cave) visit — largest in Nepal
  • Afternoon cooking class with Newari family
  • Evening walk to the sky view cafe
🏨 Bandipur Heritage Guesthouse
D3
Bandipur surrounds

Walk to Ghyalchok Village

⏰ 4 hours walking

A half-day village walk into the hills around Bandipur — through rice terraces, small settlements, and viewpoints that no standard visitor ever reaches. Return for a late lunch and afternoon rest.

  • Morning village walk to Ghyalchok
  • Terraced hillside farming landscape
  • Local school visit en route
  • Wild flower meadows and ridge trail
  • Return to Bandipur for late lunch
  • Afternoon rest and journaling
🏨 Bandipur Heritage Guesthouse
D4
Bandipur → Gorkha

Drive to Gorkha

⏰ 2 hours drive

Morning drive to Gorkha — home of Nepal's royal dynasty and the ancestral seat of Prithvi Narayan Shah, who unified Nepal in the 18th century. Afternoon ascent to the palace on the ridge above town.

  • Morning drive Bandipur to Gorkha (2hrs)
  • Gorkha orientation walk in the lower town
  • Afternoon climb to Gorkha Royal Palace (1,524m)
  • Gorkha Durbar — extraordinary hilltop palace
  • Panoramic views of Manaslu and Ganesh Himal
  • Royal history storytelling with local guide
🏨 Gorkha Guesthouse
D5
Gorkha

Gorkha Museum & Village Walk

⏰ Full day

Morning: the Gorkha Museum charting the history of Nepal's unification. Afternoon: a walk to the village of Upallo Durbar and the extraordinary hilltop temple of Gorakhnath — where the Gurkha soldiers' patron deity is enshrined.

  • Morning: Gorkha Museum — unification of Nepal
  • Gorakhnath temple and Gorakhkali shrine
  • Upallo Durbar hilltop village walk
  • Panoramic Himalayan viewpoint (clear days: 8,000m peaks)
  • Local raksi and chang tasting with elders
  • Evening cultural storytelling session
🏨 Gorkha Guesthouse
D6
Gorkha → Kathmandu

Return Kathmandu — Depart

⏰ 4 hours drive

Morning drive back to Kathmandu via the Prithvi Highway. Arrival midday — time for lunch, a final Thamel walk, and airport transfers. Certificate presentation at a lakeside café.

  • 4-hour drive back to Kathmandu
  • Lunch in Thamel
  • Final shopping and browsing
  • Certificate and group photo presentation
  • Airport transfers at departure times
