Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026. This policy explains how Unique Gap Years collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

1. Who We Are

Unique Gap Years is a travel company headquartered in Germany. Our contact email is jack.anderson@uniquegapyears.com. We are the data controller for personal data collected through our website and booking process.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact details: Name, email address, phone number
  • Booking information: Travel dates, group size, destination preferences
  • Health information: Dietary requirements, medical conditions relevant to your tour (only where you voluntarily provide this)
  • Payment information: Processed securely via Formspree — we do not store card details
  • Communications: Emails, enquiry form submissions, and WhatsApp messages

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and manage your booking
  • Communicate with you about your tour or placement
  • Provide pre-departure information and support
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Send occasional updates about new tours (only with your consent — unsubscribe any time)

4. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your data only with parties necessary to deliver your tour: local ground operators, accommodation providers, and our in-country guides. We do not sell your data to third parties or share it for marketing purposes. All third parties are required to handle your data in accordance with applicable data protection law.

5. Data Retention

We retain booking data for 7 years in accordance with financial record-keeping requirements. Communication records are retained for 3 years. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing us — subject to legal retention requirements.

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (where legally permitted)
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

7. Cookies

Our website uses no tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts. The only external services our site contacts are Google Maps (for tour location maps) and Formspree (for booking form submissions).

8. Contact

To exercise your rights or ask questions about this policy, contact jack.anderson@uniquegapyears.com
