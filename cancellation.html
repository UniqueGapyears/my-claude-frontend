How to Cancel

All cancellations must be made in writing by email to jack.anderson@uniquegapyears.com. The date of cancellation is the date we receive your written notice. We will acknowledge your cancellation within 24 hours and confirm any refund due.

Volunteer Placements and Au Pair

For volunteer placements and au pair programmes, the same cancellation policy applies to the placement fee. Where a specific date has been agreed with a host school or family, additional non-recoverable costs may apply — these will be communicated clearly at the time of booking.

If We Cancel

If Unique Gap Years cancels your tour for any reason within our control, you will receive a full refund of all monies paid. If the cancellation is due to force majeure (natural disaster, political instability, pandemic, etc.), we will offer a full credit note valid for 24 months, or a refund minus any non-recoverable costs already incurred on your behalf.

Travel Insurance

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance that includes cancellation cover at the time of booking. This protects you against the loss of your tour cost in the event of illness, injury, or other unforeseen circumstances. We can recommend reputable travel insurance providers on request.

Questions about cancellation?

Contact Jack directly — we always try to be as flexible as possible and find a solution that works for both parties.
