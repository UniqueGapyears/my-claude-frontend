Overview

About This Tour

Canoe through Chitwan's river channels, track rhino and elephant on foot with a Tharu guide, and sleep in a community-owned jungle lodge far from the resort crowds. This is wildlife immersion at its most authentic.

Highlights

Why This Tour

🦏

Rhino Tracking on Foot

Walk through the jungle with an expert Tharu tracker to observe one-horned rhinos in their natural habitat.

🛶

Dugout Canoe Safari

Glide silently along the Rapti River at dusk, spotting crocodiles, otters, and migratory birds.

🏘️

Tharu Cultural Evening

Experience traditional Tharu stick dancing, food, and stories around the fire with a local family.

Day by Day

Your Full Itinerary

All times are approximate. Flexibility is built into every day — your guide will adapt based on conditions, wildlife sightings, and group preference.

D1
Kathmandu

Arrival in Kathmandu

⏰ Full day

Arrive in Kathmandu and meet your guide at Tribhuvan International Airport. Transfer to your guesthouse in Thamel for an orientation walk, team briefing, and welcome dinner.

  • Airport pickup and hotel transfer
  • Welcome briefing and kit check
  • Thamel orientation walk
  • Welcome dinner with your guide group
🏨 Thamel Guesthouse, Kathmandu
D2
Kathmandu → Chitwan

Drive to Chitwan

⏰ 6–7 hours drive

Early morning departure by private vehicle through the Terai lowlands. Arrive at our community jungle lodge by early afternoon, settle in, and take an evening canoe safari on the Rapti River.

  • 6am departure from Kathmandu
  • Scenic drive through the Terai Plains
  • Arrival at community jungle lodge
  • Evening dugout canoe safari on the Rapti River
  • Crocodile and bird spotting at dusk
🏨 Community Jungle Lodge, Chitwan
D3
Chitwan National Park

Jungle Walk & Rhino Tracking

⏰ Full day

The highlight of the safari — a guided walk into the jungle interior with an expert Tharu naturalist. Track one-horned rhinos and deer through the elephant grass, and visit a watchtower for panoramic views of the park.

  • 5:30am wake-up call and jungle breakfast
  • 3-hour guided jungle walk with Tharu tracker
  • One-horned rhino tracking in elephant grass
  • Watchtower visit for panoramic wildlife views
  • Afternoon bird watching along the river
  • Evening Tharu cultural programme
🏨 Community Jungle Lodge, Chitwan
D4
Chitwan & Sauraha

Elephant Observation & Village Life

⏰ Full day

Visit the elephant breeding centre in the morning (observation only — no riding), then spend the afternoon in a Tharu village learning about traditional medicine, farming, and daily life away from tourist areas.

  • Morning: elephant breeding centre (observation only)
  • Tharu village cultural walk
  • Traditional Tharu lunch with a local family
  • Medicinal plant demonstration in the forest
  • Evening stick dancing performance
🏨 Community Jungle Lodge, Chitwan
D5
Chitwan → Kathmandu

Return to Kathmandu

⏰ Return journey

Morning bird walk along the Rapti River at sunrise, then breakfast and departure. Return drive to Kathmandu with time for farewell dinner and a debrief with your guide on everything you have experienced.

  • Dawn bird walk along the Rapti River
  • Farewell breakfast at the lodge
  • Drive back to Kathmandu
  • Evening farewell dinner in Thamel
  • Tour debrief and certificate presentation
🏨 Departure or onward travel
