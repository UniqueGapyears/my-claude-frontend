Southern Africa Highlights

The Golden Triangle & Beyond

Our Southern Africa programme weaves together the region's greatest natural wonders with authentic local experiences.

Victoria Falls

Witness one of the world's largest waterfalls from both the Zambian and Zimbabwean sides, with optional helicopter rides for a breathtaking aerial perspective.

Chobe & Zambezi

Boat safaris through Chobe National Park — home to Africa's largest elephant population — and unforgettable sunset cruises on the Zambezi River.

Kruger National Park

Experience South Africa's iconic wilderness, encounter the Big Five, and taste exotic African cuisine on guided game drives and bush walks.

Soweto & Township Culture

Guided township tours offering an honest, fascinating window into South African history, resilience, and vibrant contemporary urban life.

Garden Route

Adventures along South Africa's stunning Garden Route with hostel stays via Bazbus — coastlines, forests, whale-watching and adventure sports.

Eswatini & Lesotho

Explore two extraordinary mountain kingdoms, each with deep cultural traditions, stunning highland landscapes, wildlife reserves and royal ceremonies.
