Overview

About This Tour

Big Five game drives in Kruger, wine tasting in Stellenbosch, and a Table Mountain sunset in Cape Town. South Africa's greatest hits, done properly — with local guides, community visits, and time to understand what you're seeing.

Highlights

Why This Tour

🦁

Big Five in Kruger

South Africa's flagship national park — lion, leopard, elephant, rhino, and buffalo in one of Africa's greatest wildlife reserves.

🍷

Stellenbosch Winelands

Tour the Winelands with a local guide — not just tasting rooms but the history, the farmworker stories, and the vineyards of the Cape.

🏔️

Table Mountain

Take the cable car or hike — either way, you're on one of the world's great iconic summits with views across two oceans.

Day by Day

Your Full Itinerary

All times are approximate. Flexibility is built into every day — your guide will adapt based on conditions, wildlife sightings, and group preference.

D1
Johannesburg

Arrive Johannesburg

⏰ Day 1

Arrive at OR Tambo and transfer to Soweto for an evening orientation walk — one of the most historically significant urban areas in the world.

  • Airport pickup
  • Soweto orientation walk
  • Vilakazi Street — Mandela and Tutu homes
  • Welcome dinner in Soweto
🏨 Soweto Guesthouse
D2
Soweto, Johannesburg

Soweto Deep Dive

⏰ Day 2

Full guided Soweto tour — Apartheid Museum, Hector Pieterson Memorial, and an honest conversation about post-Apartheid South Africa with a local guide who grew up here.

  • Apartheid Museum (morning)
  • Hector Pieterson Memorial and Museum
  • Vilakazi Street and local lunch
  • Community discussion with local guide
  • Orlando Towers and Soweto skyline
🏨 Soweto Guesthouse
D3
Johannesburg → Hazyview

Drive to Kruger — Hazyview

⏰ Day 3

Drive northeast through the Lowveld to the gateway towns of Kruger. Afternoon arrival, settle in, and evening game drive along the boundary.

  • Morning drive to Hazyview (5hrs)
  • Arrival at safari camp
  • Afternoon rest and orientation
  • Evening boundary game drive
🏨 Hazyview Safari Camp
D4
Kruger National Park

Kruger Game Drive Day 1

⏰ Day 4 — Full Day

First full day in Kruger. Dawn and dusk game drives with a ranger. The southern section is renowned for lion, leopard, and cheetah sightings.

  • 5am dawn game drive (3hrs)
  • Big Five tracking with park ranger
  • Midday rest at watering hole
  • 4pm afternoon game drive
  • Lion sighting (high probability this section)
  • Night sounds experience from camp
🏨 Kruger Safari Camp
D5
Kruger National Park

Kruger Game Drive Day 2

⏰ Day 5

Second Kruger day — different route, different ecosystem. Drive north toward the central Kruger where elephant herds and buffalo are most concentrated.

  • Dawn game drive — central Kruger
  • Elephant herd tracking (near-certain sighting)
  • Waterhole vehicle stop and wildlife watch
  • Afternoon game walk with armed ranger
  • Leopard territory tracking
🏨 Kruger Safari Camp
D6
Hazyview → Cape Town → Stellenbosch

Fly Cape Town — Arrive Winelands

⏰ Day 6

Morning drive back to Johannesburg and flight to Cape Town. Afternoon drive to Stellenbosch through the Winelands valleys for first views of the Cape mountains.

  • Morning drive to Johannesburg airport
  • Internal flight Cape Town (2hrs)
  • Drive to Stellenbosch through Winelands
  • Check in at Winelands B&B
  • Evening walk through Stellenbosch town
🏨 Stellenbosch B&B
D7
Stellenbosch & Franschhoek

Winelands Deep Dive

⏰ Day 7 — Full Day

A full day in the Winelands with a local guide who knows the history — from the Huguenot heritage of Franschhoek to the farmworker stories that most wine tourists never hear.

  • Morning: Stellenbosch winery tour with tasting
  • Farmworker history and community visit
  • Drive to Franschhoek Pass viewpoint
  • Franschhoek village and wine tasting
  • Huguenot monument and museum
  • Evening dinner in Stellenbosch
🏨 Stellenbosch B&B
D8
Stellenbosch → Cape Town

Drive to Cape Town — Arrival

⏰ Day 8

Drive to Cape Town via the scenic Chapman's Peak route. Afternoon arrival — first views of Table Mountain and the Atlantic seaboard.

  • Chapman's Peak Drive — one of world's great coastal roads
  • Hout Bay harbour visit
  • Cape Town arrival and apartment check-in
  • Evening V&A Waterfront walk
🏨 Cape Town Apartment
D9
Cape Town

Robben Island

⏰ Day 9

Morning ferry to Robben Island — where Nelson Mandela spent 18 of his 27 prison years. Guided by a former political prisoner. The most powerful few hours of the entire trip.

  • 9am ferry to Robben Island (45min)
  • Tour guided by former political prisoner
  • Nelson Mandela's cell (B Section)
  • Lime quarry where prisoners worked
  • Afternoon: Bo-Kaap neighbourhood walk
  • Cape Malay community and history
🏨 Cape Town Apartment
D10
Cape Town

Table Mountain

⏰ Day 10

Table Mountain — either the cable car up or a 3-hour hike, depending on the group's fitness. Summit views across two oceans, the Cape Peninsula, and the city. Afternoon: Long Street and vibrant Cape Town food scene.

  • 8am: Table Mountain hike or cable car
  • Summit plateau exploration (2hrs)
  • Views of Cape Point and both oceans
  • Afternoon: Long Street neighbourhood
  • Cape Malay food tour in Bo-Kaap
🏨 Cape Town Apartment
D11
Cape Peninsula

Cape Peninsula Tour

⏰ Day 11

Full-day Cape Peninsula tour — Cape Point (the meeting of two oceans), Boulders Beach penguins, and the wild Atlantic coast. One of the world's great drive routes.

  • Cape Point National Park
  • Cape of Good Hope (southwesternmost tip of Africa)
  • Boulders Beach — African penguin colony
  • Simonstown harbour and naval history
  • Chapman's Peak return drive
  • Farewell dinner at the V&A Waterfront
🏨 Cape Town Apartment
D12
Cape Town

Departure Cape Town

⏰ Day 12

Final morning in Cape Town — last look at Table Mountain, breakfast in De Waterkant, and transfer to Cape Town International Airport.

  • Final Table Mountain viewpoint breakfast
  • Debrief and certificate presentation
  • Airport transfer
  • International departures
🏨 Departure
