Overview

About This Tour

Teach English or run sports coaching sessions in a Harare community school, with a weekend Victoria Falls trip included. A genuine, long-form volunteer experience that creates real educational impact — not a tourist activity.

Highlights

Why This Tour

📚

Real Teaching Impact

You will be assigned to a specific class and teacher for the full placement duration — genuine curriculum contribution, not a drop-in.

⚽

Sports Coaching

Football, netball, athletics, and cricket coaching sessions for students who have very little access to organised sports programmes.

💧

Weekend at Victoria Falls

Every volunteer placement includes a weekend trip to Victoria Falls — both sides, Zambezi cruise, and a village homestay.

Day by Day

Your Full Itinerary

All times approximate. Your guide adapts each day to conditions, wildlife, and group preference.

D1
Harare, Zimbabwe

Arrival & Orientation

⏰ Week 1, Day 1

Arrive in Harare. Full volunteer orientation — school introduction, class assignment, teaching resources, cultural briefing, and a tour of your neighbourhood with the volunteer coordinator.

  • Airport pickup by volunteer coordinator
  • Volunteer house check-in
  • Full orientation briefing
  • School visit and teacher introduction
  • Neighbourhood orientation walk
  • Welcome dinner with volunteer team
🏨 Volunteer House, Harare
D2
Community School, Harare

First Week Teaching

⏰ Week 1, Mon–Fri

Your first week in the classroom — supported by your assigned teacher, developing your lessons, building relationships with students, and finding your rhythm. After-school sports coaching sessions on Tuesday and Thursday.

  • 7:30am: school arrival and morning assembly
  • 8am–1pm: morning teaching sessions (English/literacy)
  • Lunchtime: student mentoring and interaction
  • 2pm: afternoon sessions or sports coaching
  • Friday: first week reflection with your coordinator
  • Weekend: Harare city exploration with fellow volunteers
🏨 Volunteer House, Harare
D3
Harare & surrounds

Ongoing Placement Weeks

⏰ Weeks 2–[duration]

The rhythm of a teaching placement — morning lessons, afternoon coaching, weekend excursions to local markets, craft villages, and the botanical garden. Your impact deepens week by week.

  • Daily morning teaching sessions
  • Twice-weekly sports coaching
  • Weekly coordinator check-in
  • Weekend cultural and community activities
  • Optional evening Shona language lessons
  • Ongoing student project development
🏨 Volunteer House, Harare
D4
Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe

Victoria Falls Weekend

⏰ Included weekend trip

Every volunteer placement includes a Victoria Falls trip — flights or coach depending on duration, two nights at a guesthouse, both sides of the Falls, and a Zambezi sunset cruise.

  • Travel to Victoria Falls
  • Zimbabwe side rainforest walk (all viewpoints)
  • Zambian side crossing — Knife Edge Bridge
  • Zambezi sunset cruise — hippos and elephants
  • Village homestay dinner
  • Return to Harare
🏨 Victoria Falls Guesthouse (included)
D5
Harare, Zimbabwe

Final Week & Farewell

⏰ Final week

Final teaching week — often the most meaningful as you see the progress students have made. Handover to your replacement or teacher. School farewell assembly and certificate presentation. Coordinator debrief and departure.

  • Final teaching sessions and student presentations
  • School farewell assembly
  • Student certificate ceremony
  • Coordinator debrief session
  • Farewell dinner with volunteer team
  • Airport transfer and departure
