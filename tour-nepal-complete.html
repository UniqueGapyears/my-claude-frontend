About This Tour

Chitwan jungle safari → Pokhara → Annapurna hidden villages → Mustang → Lumbini → Kathmandu. The complete Nepal experience across five regions in three weeks — designed to fit a university summer break and cover every dimension of this extraordinary country.

Highlights

Why This Tour

Every transition is smooth, every day purposeful. Three weeks that feel like a semester's worth of education — and adventure.

Day by Day

Your Full Itinerary

All times approximate. Your guide adapts each day to conditions, wildlife, and group preference.

D1
Kathmandu

Arrival Kathmandu

⏰ Day 1

Arrive and meet your guide. Full programme briefing, permit processing, and an evening walk through Thamel to begin to get your bearings.

  • Airport pickup
  • Programme briefing and permit processing
  • Thamel orientation walk
  • Welcome dinner
🏨 Thamel Guesthouse
D2
Kathmandu Valley

Kathmandu Orientation

⏰ Day 2

Full Kathmandu orientation — old city hidden courtyards, Boudhanath Stupa, and a Patan afternoon with your guide.

  • Old city bahal courtyard walk
  • Boudhanath Stupa circumambulation
  • Patan Durbar Square afternoon
  • Local Newari dinner
🏨 Thamel Guesthouse
D3
Kathmandu → Chitwan

Drive to Chitwan

⏰ Days 3–4 transit + 2 nights

Drive to Chitwan National Park and spend two nights at the community jungle lodge — rhino tracking, canoe safari, and Tharu cultural evening.

  • Drive to Chitwan (5hrs)
  • Evening canoe safari on the Rapti River
  • Day 4: jungle walk with Tharu tracker — rhino tracking
  • Tharu cultural evening programme
🏨 Community Jungle Lodge, Chitwan
D4
Chitwan → Pokhara

Drive to Pokhara

⏰ Day 5

Drive from Chitwan to Pokhara — a beautiful journey through the Terai and Himalayan foothills. Arrive at the lakeside for a sunset over Phewa Lake.

  • Morning drive Chitwan to Pokhara (4hrs)
  • Arrival at Phewa Lake
  • Lakeside evening walk
  • Sunset over the Annapurna range
🏨 Pokhara Lakeside Guesthouse
D5
Pokhara

Pokhara — Sarangkot & Kayak

⏰ Days 6–7

Two nights in Pokhara — Sarangkot sunrise on Day 6, kayaking on Phewa Lake, World Peace Pagoda hike on Day 7, and the old bazaar.

  • Day 6: 4:30am Sarangkot sunrise hike
  • Dawn kayak on Phewa Lake
  • Day 7: World Peace Pagoda forest trail
  • Pokhara old bazaar and temple walk
🏨 Pokhara Lakeside Guesthouse
D6
Pokhara → Annapurna foothills

Annapurna Hidden Villages

⏰ Days 8–11 trekking

Four days in the Annapurna foothills — trekking to Ghalegaun and Sikles, staying in village homestays, and seeing the Himalayas from angles no guidebook shows.

  • Day 8: drive to Khudi trailhead, trek to Ghalegaun
  • Day 9: Ghalegaun exploration and farm visit
  • Day 10: trek through rhododendron forest to Sikles
  • Day 11: ridgeline walk, descent to Rupa Lake
🏨 Village Homestays (Ghalegaun then Sikles)
D7
Pokhara → Mustang

Fly Pokhara → Jomsom — Enter Mustang

⏰ Days 12–15

Four extraordinary days in Upper Mustang — domestic flight to Jomsom, trekking through ancient canyon landscapes to Lo Manthang, the walled capital of the forbidden kingdom.

  • Day 12: flight to Jomsom, trek to Kagbeni
  • Day 13: enter Upper Mustang — trek to Chele
  • Day 14: Chhoser cave monasteries, trek to Syangboche
  • Day 15: arrive Lo Manthang — the walled city
🏨 Lo Manthang Guesthouse
D8
Mustang → Jomsom → Kathmandu

Lo Manthang & Return

⏰ Days 16–17

A free day in Lo Manthang exploring the medieval city, then the return flight to Kathmandu via Jomsom and Pokhara.

  • Day 16: Lo Manthang free exploration, royal palace
  • Day 17: fly Jomsom → Pokhara → Kathmandu
  • Afternoon rest in Kathmandu
🏨 Kathmandu Guesthouse
D9
Kathmandu → Lumbini

Fly to Lumbini

⏰ Days 18–19

Two days at Lumbini — the birthplace of the Buddha. Dawn meditation in the sacred garden, the Maya Devi Temple, the international monastery zone, and a trip to Kapilavastu.

  • Day 18: fly Kathmandu → Bhairahawa, transfer Lumbini
  • Maya Devi Temple and sacred garden
  • Dawn meditation with resident monk
  • Day 19: international monastery zone walk
  • Kapilavastu ancient ruins day trip
🏨 Monastery Guesthouse, Lumbini
D10
Lumbini → Kathmandu

Return Kathmandu — Final Day

⏰ Day 20

Return flight to Kathmandu. Free afternoon for final shopping and reflection. Evening: grand celebration dinner with the full group and guide team — certificate presentations.

  • Morning flight back to Kathmandu
  • Free afternoon — Thamel and Boudhanath
  • Grand farewell dinner
  • Certificate and photo presentation
🏨 Kathmandu Guesthouse
D11
Kathmandu

Departure

⏰ Day 21

Final breakfast together. Individual airport transfers for international departures. Nepal Complete — finished.

  • Final breakfast
  • Airport transfers
  • International departures
🏨 Departure
