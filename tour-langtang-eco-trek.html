Overview

About This Tour

Trek through Langtang National Park — one of Nepal's most ecologically rich regions — meeting Tamang communities who are rebuilding with extraordinary resilience after the 2015 earthquake. A journey that is deeply human as well as breathtakingly beautiful.

Highlights

Why This Tour

🌿

Incredible Biodiversity

From subtropical forest to alpine meadow in three days — red panda, Himalayan tahr, langur monkeys, and rare birds in a single national park.

🏔️

Langtang Lirung Views

Trek directly beneath Langtang Lirung (7,227m) — views that most trekkers in Nepal never access.

🤝

Tamang Community Rebuild

Meet the Tamang families who lost everything in 2015 and have rebuilt their communities with astonishing spirit — their stories will stay with you.

Day by Day

Your Full Itinerary

All times are approximate. Flexibility is built into every day — your guide will adapt based on conditions, wildlife sightings, and group preference.

D1
Kathmandu

Kathmandu Arrival & Briefing

⏰ Full day

Arrive in Kathmandu. Met by guide, permits processed. Ecological briefing — conservation context for Langtang and the 2015 earthquake. Evening walk to Boudhanath.

  • Airport pickup and permit processing
  • Conservation and ecology briefing
  • Earthquake history and Tamang community context
  • Boudhanath Stupa evening walk
  • Group dinner and route introduction
🏨 Kathmandu Guesthouse
D2
Kathmandu → Syabrubesi

Drive to Syabrubesi

⏰ 7 hours drive

Drive north along the Trishuli River into the mountains. Arrive in Syabrubesi (1,550m) — the gateway to Langtang. Evening walk along the river.

  • 7-hour scenic mountain drive
  • Trishuli River gorge views
  • Checkpoint entry into Langtang National Park
  • Syabrubesi village walk
  • Evening meal and early night
🏨 Syabrubesi Teahouse
D3
Syabrubesi → Lama Hotel

Trek to Lama Hotel

⏰ 5–6 hours trekking

Enter the forest and begin the ascent alongside the Langtang Khola. Dense subtropical jungle gives way to oak and rhododendron — perfect red panda habitat. First views of snow peaks appear through the canopy.

  • Enter Langtang valley forest
  • Red panda habitat zone observation
  • Subtropical to temperate forest transition
  • Waterfall rest stop
  • Lama Hotel arrival (2,380m)
🏨 Lama Hotel Teahouse
D4
Lama Hotel → Langtang Village

Trek to Langtang Village

⏰ 5 hours trekking

Trek through some of the most dramatic scenery in Nepal — Himalayan tahr on the rocky slopes, langur monkeys in the canopy, and the first sight of Langtang Lirung dominating the valley head.

  • Himalayan tahr spotting on rocky slopes
  • Langur monkey observation
  • Ghodatabela viewpoint (3,030m)
  • Langtang Village arrival (3,430m)
  • Meeting with village community leader
  • 2015 earthquake memorial visit
🏨 Langtang Village Teahouse
D5
Langtang Village

Tamang Heritage Community Day

⏰ Full day

A day dedicated to understanding the Tamang community's story. Guided by a local elder, visit rebuilt homes, the new community school, and the memorial to those lost in 2015. Afternoon: yak cheese factory visit.

  • Community walk with Tamang elder
  • Rebuilt village and community school visit
  • 2015 earthquake story and rebuilding journey
  • Yak cheese factory tour and tasting
  • Traditional Tamang meal
  • Evening cultural storytelling
🏨 Langtang Village Teahouse
D6
Langtang → Kyanjin Gompa

Trek to Kyanjin Gompa

⏰ 3 hours trekking

Short but spectacular walk to Kyanjin Gompa at 3,870m. Afternoon optional climb to Kyanjin Ri (4,773m) for 360-degree views of Langtang Lirung, Dorje Lakpa, and the entire Langtang range.

  • Morning trek to Kyanjin Gompa (3,870m)
  • Visit to the ancient Kyanjin Gompa monastery
  • Afternoon optional: Kyanjin Ri summit (4,773m)
  • 360-degree Himalayan panorama
  • Alpine meadow and glacier views
  • Evening acclimatisation rest
🏨 Kyanjin Gompa Teahouse
D7
Kyanjin → Syabrubesi

Descend to Syabrubesi

⏰ 6–7 hours descent

Long but satisfying descent the full length of the valley. Different light, different perspective — the valley reveals new layers on the way down. Final night at Syabrubesi.

  • Full valley descent along the Langtang Khola
  • Bird identification walk with guide
  • Forest photography session
  • Return to Syabrubesi
  • Celebration dinner and debrief
🏨 Syabrubesi Teahouse
D8
Syabrubesi → Kathmandu

Return to Kathmandu

⏰ 7 hours drive

Return drive to Kathmandu. Afternoon free for shopping or Boudhanath visit. Final group dinner and certificate presentation.

  • Morning drive back to Kathmandu
  • Afternoon free time in Thamel
  • Optional Boudhanath final visit
  • Group farewell dinner
  • Certificate and photo presentation
🏨 Kathmandu Guesthouse
D9
Kathmandu

Departure

⏰ Morning

Final morning in Kathmandu. Breakfast together, last-minute shopping in Thamel, and airport transfers at your departure time.

  • Final breakfast together
  • Last-minute Thamel browsing
  • Airport transfer at departure time
  • International onward flights
🏨 Departure
