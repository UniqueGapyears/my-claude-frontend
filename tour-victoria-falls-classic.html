Overview

About This Tour

Both sides of the Falls, a Zambezi sunset cruise, a village homestay, and a guided rainforest walk through the spray. Victoria Falls is one of the world's greatest natural spectacles — this is the definitive way to experience it properly.

Highlights

Why This Tour

💧

Both Sides of the Falls

Experience Victoria Falls from both Zimbabwe and Zambia — two completely different perspectives on the world's largest curtain of falling water.

🚤

Zambezi Sunset Cruise

Watch the sun drop behind the Zambian banks from the deck of a riverboat, with hippos, elephants, and crocodiles along the shoreline.

🏡

Village Homestay

Spend a night with a local family in the villages above the gorge — share a meal, help with cooking, and hear stories that no hotel could ever offer.

Day by Day

Your Full Itinerary

All times are approximate. Flexibility is built into every day — your guide will adapt based on conditions, wildlife sightings, and group preference.

D1
Victoria Falls Town, Zimbabwe

Arrival in Victoria Falls

⏰ Full day

Arrive at Victoria Falls Airport and meet your guide. Check into the guesthouse and take your first afternoon walk to the rim of the Falls. The spray cloud visible from miles away will become your constant companion.

  • Airport pickup and guesthouse check-in
  • Orientation walk with local guide
  • First views of Victoria Falls from the Zimbabwe rim
  • Sunset at the Falls lookout point
  • Welcome dinner at a local restaurant
🏨 Victoria Falls Guesthouse
D2
Victoria Falls National Park, Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe Rainforest Walk

⏰ Full day

Enter the Victoria Falls National Park rainforest — created entirely by the spray of the Falls — with your guide. Walk the full length of the Zimbabwe rim through dripping forest to 16 viewpoints. The roar and mist are astonishing.

  • National Park rainforest walk (8am entry)
  • 16 viewpoints along the Zimbabwe rim
  • Danger Point — standing above the gorge
  • Livingstone statue and historical context
  • Devil's Pool from the rim view
  • Afternoon at leisure in Falls town
🏨 Victoria Falls Guesthouse
D3
Zambezi River

Zambezi Sunset Cruise

⏰ Afternoon/evening

Morning free for optional activities (helicopter, bungee, white water rafting — bookable locally). Afternoon: the iconic Zambezi sunset cruise — 2.5 hours on the river watching wildlife from the deck as the sun sets over Zambia.

  • Morning free for optional adventure activities
  • 2pm: board the Zambezi River cruise boat
  • 2.5 hours on the upper Zambezi
  • Hippo pod observation (multiple pods guaranteed)
  • Elephant and crocodile sightings along the bank
  • Sundowner drinks as the sun sets
  • Return to Victoria Falls town
🏨 Victoria Falls Guesthouse
D4
Livingstone, Zambia

Cross to Zambia — Livingstone Side

⏰ Full day

Cross the border into Zambia and visit Victoria Falls from the Zambian side — widely considered the more dramatic view. Then explore Livingstone town, the David Livingstone Museum, and the edge of the gorge at Knife Edge Bridge.

  • Border crossing to Zambia (by foot across the bridge)
  • Zambian side Falls viewpoints
  • Knife Edge Bridge — suspended above the gorge
  • David Livingstone Museum and history
  • Livingstone town exploration
  • Return to Zimbabwe side
🏨 Victoria Falls Guesthouse
D5
Villages above the gorge

Village Homestay — Community Life

⏰ Full day

Leave the tourist area and spend the day in the villages that have existed alongside the Falls for generations. Help with cooking, visit the community school, and settle in for an overnight homestay.

  • Morning drive to local community village
  • Community school visit and classroom session
  • Cooking lesson with local family (traditional sadza)
  • Village life immersion — farming and daily routine
  • Meeting with community leader
  • Overnight with host family — shared stories
🏨 Village Homestay
D6
Zambezi River (upper section)

Dugout Canoe Safari

⏰ Morning

Early morning dugout canoe safari on the upper Zambezi — the section above the Falls where the river is calm. Different entirely from yesterday's boat — silent, close to the water, and extraordinary for wildlife.

  • 6am: dugout canoe launch on the upper Zambezi
  • Silent wildlife observation from the canoe
  • Hippo channels and bird-rich islands
  • Crocodile sunbathing observation
  • Return to Victoria Falls town
  • Afternoon free for rest or optional activities
🏨 Victoria Falls Guesthouse
D7
Victoria Falls

Final Morning & Departure

⏰ Morning

Final morning walk to the Falls at sunrise — the most peaceful time. Farewell breakfast with your guide, airport transfer, and onward travel.

  • Sunrise walk to Victoria Falls (6am)
  • Farewell breakfast with local guide
  • Final shopping at the craft market
  • Airport transfer
  • Onward flights
