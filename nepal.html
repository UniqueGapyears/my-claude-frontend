The Hidden Corners

Nepal Beyond the Guidebook

The places, people and traditions most travellers never reach — and the ones our students talk about for years afterwards.

🦏

Chitwan's Inner Jungle

Beyond the resort safaris — deep jungle with Tharu trackers where rhino, gharial and Bengal tigers still roam.

🏔️

Upper Mustang

The forbidden kingdom — ancient cave monasteries and Tibetan Buddhist culture largely unchanged for centuries.

☸️

Lumbini — Buddha's Birthplace

UNESCO World Heritage Site — monasteries from 20 nations encircle the sacred Maya Devi temple. Serene and profound.

💎

Shey Phoksundo Lake

One of the most spectacular alpine lakes on earth — turquoise waters surrounded by thousand-metre cliffs in total silence.

🏘️

Hidden Annapurna Villages

Ghalegaun, Sikles, Ghaletar — settlements that see a fraction of circuit traffic, with extraordinary highland hospitality.

🏛️

Bandipur Old Town

A perfectly preserved hilltop Newari town — cobbled streets, traditional courtyards, and no through traffic.

👑

Gorkha Royal Palace

The ancestral home of Nepal's royal dynasty — a stunning hilltop palace above the clouds, barely visited.

🌾

Terai Plains & Borderlands

Nepal's tropical south — dense forests, rice fields, elephant corridors, and warm Tharu community hospitality.
