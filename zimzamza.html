Zimbabwe

Victoria Falls & the Soul of Southern Africa

Zimbabwe is a country of extraordinary contrasts — from the thundering spectacle of Victoria Falls to quiet village life in the highlands. Our programmes go beyond the waterfall to discover culture, people, and wildlife.

  • Victoria Falls — explored from both the Zambian and Zimbabwean sides
  • Zambezi River — sunset cruises, canoe safaris, and riverside wildlife
  • Harare — arts, sculpture parks, markets, and community school visits
  • Volunteer teaching and sports coaching placements
  • Village homestays and cultural exchange in rural communities

Zambia

The Golden Triangle & the Zambezi

Zambia is home to some of Africa most spectacular wildlife and the Zambian side of Victoria Falls is, many say, the more dramatic of the two. Our programmes combine world-class safari with genuine community engagement.

  • Chobe National Park — home to Africa largest elephant population
  • The Zambezi — mokoro canoe, sunset cruises, and walking safaris
  • Livingstone — the adventure capital of Southern Africa
  • Rural volunteer teaching placements in community schools
  • The Golden Triangle — Vic Falls, Chobe, and the Zambezi in one safari

South Africa

Kruger, Cape Town & the Garden Route

South Africa is one of the world most diverse destinations — Big Five safaris, wine country, the spectacular Cape Peninsula, Soweto powerful history, and the stunning coastal Garden Route.

  • Kruger National Park — Big Five game drives with expert rangers
  • Cape Town — Table Mountain, Robben Island, the Cape Peninsula
  • Soweto — one of Africa most important cultural and historical journeys
  • Garden Route — whale watching, forests, and coastal adventure
  • Volunteer teaching and au pair placements in Cape Town and Johannesburg
