Overview

About This Tour

Kayak on Phewa Lake at dawn, hike to Sarangkot for Himalayan sunrise views, and discover the villages and sacred sites around the valley that tourists rush straight past. Pokhara rewards those who slow down and look beyond the lakeside cafes.

Highlights

Why This Tour

🚣

Dawn Kayak on Phewa Lake

Paddle out on Phewa Lake before sunrise — the Annapurna range reflected on the still water is one of Nepal's most magical sights.

🌅

Sarangkot Sunrise

Hike to Sarangkot at 5am for sunrise over Annapurna South, Hiunchuli, Machapuchare, and Dhaulagiri — all visible from a single viewpoint.

🕍

Bindhyabasini Temple

A hilltop Hindu temple in the old bazaar, surrounded by local worshippers and offering sweeping valley views — almost never visited by tourists.

Day by Day

Your Full Itinerary

All times are approximate. Flexibility is built into every day — your guide will adapt based on conditions, wildlife sightings, and group preference.

D1
Pokhara

Arrival & Phewa Lake at Dusk

⏰ Afternoon

Arrive in Pokhara. Meet your guide at the lakeside. Afternoon at leisure — get your bearings, explore the lakeside. As the sun sets, take a rowing boat to the Tal Barahi island temple in the middle of Phewa Lake.

  • Transfer to Lakeside guesthouse
  • Orientation walk with your local guide
  • Rowing boat to Tal Barahi island temple
  • Sunset over the Annapurna range from the lake
  • Welcome dinner at a local restaurant
🏨 Lakeside Guesthouse, Pokhara
D2
Sarangkot & Phewa Lake

Sarangkot Sunrise & Kayaking

⏰ Early morning to afternoon

4:30am wake-up for the Sarangkot hike — worth every minute of lost sleep. Return for breakfast and then a 2-hour guided kayak on Phewa Lake, reaching hidden corners of the shore inaccessible by foot.

  • 4:30am departure for Sarangkot hike (1,592m)
  • Sunrise over eight Himalayan peaks
  • 1.5hr descent back to lakeside
  • Breakfast at lakeside café
  • 2-hour guided kayak on Phewa Lake
  • Hidden lakeshore coves and bird life
  • Afternoon rest and free time
🏨 Lakeside Guesthouse, Pokhara
D3
Pokhara Old Bazaar & surrounds

Old Bazaar & Hidden Temples

⏰ Full day

Leave the tourist lakeside and spend the day in the real Pokhara — the old bazaar, the Bindhyabasini temple complex, and the markets where local Newari and Gurung traders have gathered for generations.

  • Morning: Pokhara Old Bazaar walk with local guide
  • Bindhyabasini Temple — hilltop Hindu worship
  • Traditional Newari market exploration
  • Local Gurung household visit and tea
  • Afternoon: Devi Falls and Gupteshwor Cave
  • Evening: Lakeside music and food exploration
🏨 Lakeside Guesthouse, Pokhara
D4
Pokhara South & Phewa shore

World Peace Pagoda Hike & Village Walk

⏰ Full day hiking

Hike to the stunning World Peace Pagoda (Shanti Stupa) via the forest trail on the south shore of Phewa Lake — far more beautiful than the road route. Continue to the village of Pumdi Bhumdi for lunch with a local family.

  • Forest trail hike to World Peace Pagoda (1,100m)
  • Panoramic Pokhara valley and Himalayan views
  • Descent to the southern lakeshore
  • Pumdi Bhumdi village lunch with local family
  • Return kayak or rowboat across Phewa Lake
  • Evening free time on the lakeside
🏨 Lakeside Guesthouse, Pokhara
D5
Rupa Lake & Pokhara

Rupa Lake & Departure

⏰ Morning excursion

Optional early morning drive to Rupa Lake — twin to Begnas, completely undiscovered, with extraordinary bird life. Return to Pokhara for farewell lunch and onward transport to Kathmandu or airport.

  • Optional: Rupa Lake morning bird walk
  • Breakfast back in Pokhara
  • Farewell lunch with local guide
  • Onward transport to Kathmandu or airport
  • Certificate and debrief
