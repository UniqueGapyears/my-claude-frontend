Overview

About This Tour

One of the most remote destinations on earth — trek through Shey Phoksundo National Park to the extraordinary turquoise crystal lake, visiting ancient Bon monasteries and salt-trading routes unchanged for a thousand years. Dolpo is the Nepal almost nobody sees.

Highlights

Why This Tour

💎

Shey Phoksundo Lake

One of the deepest and most spectacular alpine lakes on earth — turquoise water surrounded by thousand-metre cliffs in near-total silence.

⛩️

Bon Monasteries

Ancient Bon (pre-Buddhist) monasteries that are among the oldest places of worship in the Himalaya — rarely visited and utterly extraordinary.

🧂

Salt Trade Routes

Walk the ancient trans-Himalayan salt trading routes that connected Nepal and Tibet for millennia — still used by yak caravans today.

Day by Day

Your Full Itinerary

All times approximate. Your guide adapts each day to conditions, wildlife, and group preference.

D1
Kathmandu

Kathmandu — Permits & Briefing

⏰ Full day

Arrive in Kathmandu for the expedition briefing. Dolpo requires a Restricted Area Permit and thorough preparation. Full gear check, route overview, and conservation briefing with your lead guide.

  • Airport pickup
  • Restricted Area Permit processing
  • Expedition briefing — route, altitude, safety
  • Gear check and porter introduction
  • Welcome dinner in Thamel
🏨 Thamel Guesthouse, Kathmandu
D2
Kathmandu → Nepalgunj → Juphal

Fly Nepalgunj — Juphal

⏰ Two short flights

Morning flight to Nepalgunj in the Terai lowlands, then a spectacular 45-minute mountain flight to Juphal airstrip in the Bheri River valley — one of Nepal's most dramatic approaches.

  • Early flight to Nepalgunj (1hr)
  • Connecting flight to Juphal airstrip (45min)
  • Dramatic mountain approach through Himalayan ridges
  • Arrive Dunai — Dolpo district headquarters
  • Afternoon acclimatisation walk along the Bheri River
🏨 Dunai Teahouse
D3
Dunai → Chhepka

Trek Dunai to Chhepka

⏰ 5 hours trekking

Enter Shey Phoksundo National Park and begin the ascent along the Suli Gad gorge. The trail follows a roaring river through deep canyon walls — one of Nepal's most dramatic valley approaches.

  • Shey Phoksundo National Park entry
  • Trek along the Suli Gad gorge
  • Dramatic canyon walls and waterfalls
  • First views of high Dolpo ridgelines
  • Chhepka village arrival (2,780m)
🏨 Chhepka Teahouse
D4
Chhepka → Phoksundo Lake

Trek to Phoksundo Lake

⏰ 6-7 hours trekking

The day that defines the expedition. As you climb out of the gorge, Phoksundo Lake reveals itself suddenly — an almost impossibly turquoise expanse surrounded by vertical cliffs. Most trekkers feel it is the most beautiful moment of their lives.

  • Trek through rhododendron and juniper forest
  • Emergence onto the lake shore plateau
  • First sight of Shey Phoksundo Lake (3,611m)
  • Walk the lake circuit to the village of Rigmo
  • Visit to the small local monastery
  • Evening reflections on the lake at dusk
🏨 Rigmo Village Teahouse
D5
Phoksundo Lake

Phoksundo Lake Rest Day

⏰ Free exploration

A full day at the lake — the colour changes hour by hour from emerald to cobalt to deep blue-green. Morning meditation, afternoon walks, and the extraordinary silence of being completely off the grid.

  • Dawn lake walk — extraordinary light on the water
  • Phoksundo waterfalls visit (one of Nepal's highest)
  • Walk above the lake for panoramic views
  • Afternoon rest and reading
  • Visit to the lakeside monastery
  • Photography golden hour at dusk
🏨 Rigmo Village Teahouse
D6
Phoksundo → Shey Gompa

Trek to Shey Monastery

⏰ Full day high-altitude trek

Cross the Kang La pass (5,360m) — the high point of the expedition — for a descent to the ancient Shey Gompa crystal mountain monastery, one of the most sacred Bon places in the Himalaya.

  • 4am departure for Kang La pass (5,360m)
  • High-altitude pass crossing — panoramic views
  • Descent to Shey Gompa (4,390m)
  • Visit to the ancient Shey crystal mountain monastery
  • Meeting with resident Bon monks
  • Yak caravan sighting on the trade route
🏨 Shey Gompa Camp
D7
Shey Gompa

Shey Gompa Exploration

⏰ Full rest and explore day

A full day at Shey — one of the most extraordinary places in the Himalaya. Ancient Bon art, prayer wheels, and monks who have been here for decades. The snow leopard capital of Nepal.

  • Early morning monastery prayer ceremony
  • Gompa interior — ancient Bon thangka paintings
  • Snow leopard territory walk with ranger
  • Shey plain panorama and yak herder meeting
  • Afternoon rest at high altitude
  • Stargazing from camp — extraordinary sky clarity
🏨 Shey Gompa Camp
D8
Shey → Saldang

Descend via Saldang

⏰ 6 hours descent

Trek down from the high country through dramatic landscape to Saldang — one of the largest and most traditional villages in Dolpo, with a community of Tibetan-origin farmers and an extraordinary hilltop monastery.

  • Morning descent from Shey plain
  • Views of the Tibetan plateau to the north
  • Saldang village arrival — largest in Dolpo
  • Visit to the Nanzkhang hilltop monastery
  • Community evening with Saldang families
🏨 Saldang Teahouse
D9
Saldang → Tarap

Trek to Tarap Valley

⏰ 5 hours trekking

Cross into the extraordinary Tarap Valley — a hidden hanging valley that sees perhaps a dozen trekking groups per year. Ancient chortens, mani walls, and a medieval ambience that feels utterly remote from the modern world.

  • Morning trek to Tarap Valley
  • Ancient chorten and mani wall trail
  • Tarap village arrival (3,680m)
  • Local Dolpopa family visit
  • Traditional millet chang tasting
🏨 Tarap Teahouse
D10
Tarap → Dunai

Tarap to Dunai — Final Descent

⏰ 7 hours descent

Long final trekking day — descend the full Tarap Khola valley back toward Dunai and Juphal. The lower altitude feels like oxygen after the high country. Celebration dinner in Dunai.

  • Full valley descent along Tarap Khola
  • Subtropical forest return
  • Dunai arrival — hot meal and rest
  • Celebration dinner with guide team
  • Group debrief and story sharing
🏨 Dunai Teahouse
D11
Juphal → Nepalgunj

Fly Juphal to Nepalgunj

⏰ Morning flight

Morning flight out of Dolpo — looking back down at the valleys you have walked for two weeks. Overnight in Nepalgunj before the return to Kathmandu.

  • Juphal airstrip departure
  • 45-minute mountain flight to Nepalgunj
  • Afternoon rest in Nepalgunj
  • Debrief session with lead guide
  • Group dinner
🏨 Nepalgunj Guesthouse
D12
Nepalgunj → Kathmandu

Fly Kathmandu — Rest Day

⏰ Morning flight

Return flight to Kathmandu. Full rest day — hot shower, good food, reflection. Optional Boudhanath Stupa visit for a final contemplative moment.

  • Morning flight to Kathmandu
  • Hotel check-in and long shower
  • Free day — rest and reflection
  • Optional Boudhanath Stupa visit
  • Group celebration dinner
🏨 Kathmandu Guesthouse
D13
Kathmandu

Kathmandu Free Day

⏰ Free day

A full free day in Kathmandu for shopping, Patan exploration, or simply processing the extraordinary journey you have completed. Evening: group certificate presentation and farewell dinner.

  • Free morning — Thamel shopping
  • Optional Patan Durbar Square visit
  • Certificate and photo presentation ceremony
  • Farewell dinner at a rooftop restaurant
  • Final group photograph
🏨 Kathmandu Guesthouse
D14
Kathmandu

Departure

⏰ International departure

Final breakfast together, airport transfers at individual departure times. The Dolpo expedition ends — but the memories do not.

  • Final breakfast
  • Airport transfer
  • International departures
